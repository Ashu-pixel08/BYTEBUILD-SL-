About PC Builder

Our Mission

PC Builder was created to simplify the complex process of building a custom PC. We understand that choosing the right components can be overwhelming, especially for first-time builders. Our goal is to provide an intuitive, interactive platform that guides you through every step of the PC building process, ensuring compatibility and helping you get the best value for your budget.

Our Story

PC Builder was founded in 2024 by a team of passionate PC enthusiasts who recognized the need for a better way to plan and build custom computers. After years of helping friends and family navigate the confusing world of PC components, we decided to create a tool that would make the process easier for everyone.

Today, PC Builder serves thousands of users worldwide, from first-time builders to experienced enthusiasts. Our platform continues to evolve based on user feedback, ensuring we always provide the best possible experience for our community.

Whether you're building a gaming powerhouse, a content creation workstation, or a budget-friendly home office PC, we're here to help you every step of the way.
